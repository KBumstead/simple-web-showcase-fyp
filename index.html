Welcome to the Plant Growth Predictor

The model allows plant growth prediction through time series image

About

The model is created in order to predict plant growth due to uncertainties in plant growth, which causes inefficiency in resources.

However, in some research, problems consist of noise or background clutter in the images themselves.

Objective

The objective of the model:

  • Obtaining the segmented mask of the image to proceed to the model

  • Provide the prediction mask of the plant growth itself
